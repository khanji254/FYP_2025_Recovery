\section{Testing Protocols}
\label{appendix:testing}

\textbf{Telemetry Range Test}: Transmit packets at 1 Hz, measure RSSI at 500m increments to 6 km. Success: RSSI > -100 dBm at 5 km, <5\% packet loss.

\textbf{PWM Ignition Test}: Apply PWM at 20-100\% duty cycles, record thermal profile, verify ignition. Success: controlled temperature ramp, no burnout, repeatable 10+ cycles.

\textbf{Explosion Cap Deployment}: Test charge masses (0.5-1.5g), measure deployment force with load cell, inspect parachute. Success: clean separation, zero thermal damage, 200-400 N force, consistent across 5 tests.

\textbf{Vibration/Shock Test}: Shake table 5-500 Hz sweep, 15G shock impulse, verify continuity. Success: zero component displacement, no structural failure, maintained continuity.
